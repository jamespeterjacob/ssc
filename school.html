Our Story

Smart Scholars was founded with a vision to make reading enjoyable and English language a favorite subject to children and adults through an effective and scientific methodology popularly known as Jolly Phonics, Jolly Grammar. Children would engage, explore and experience learning at its best. This helps them handle English Language effortlessly, when they not only read in English but also think and communicate in English.

Our Vision

Today a reader. Tomorrow a leader.

To empower children from all walks of life through equal and transparent access to quality education.

Our Mission

To provide quality education to all children, through experienced and well-trained teachers, via a thorough curriculum.
