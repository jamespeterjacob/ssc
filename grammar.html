Grammar

Grammar is the next stage after Phonics. This is a course which teaches students about Basic English lessons, and the ways how to use words in an appropriate sentence to make meaningful communication. Therefore, this course is packed with fun games and activities to teach essential concepts which will lead you to a very pleasant and challenging journey where you can get the experience of the process of learning English.

Age Group: 5-6 Years
Course Content

  • Parts of Speech

  • Active and Passive Voice

  • Vowel Digraphs

  • Plural Endings

  • Sentence Structure

  • Consonant Blends

  • Short Vowel Consonant Doubling
